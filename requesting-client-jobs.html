Requesting client jobs

You can request a Job by using gearman service

                        /**

                         * We do a simple call of a Job named "MmoreramerinoGearmanBundleWorkers~testB"

                         */


                        $this->getContainer()->get('gearman')->doJob('MmoreramerinoGearmanBundleWorkers~testB');

Right now only few utilities are available

                        
    
                            
  • doJob : Call the job and wait for result
    • 
                            
  • doHighJob : Call the job and wait for result on High Preference
    • 
                            
  • doLowJob : Call the job and wait for result on Low Preference
    • 
                            
  • doBackroundJob : Call the job without waitting for result.
    • It recieves a job handle for submitted job
    • 
                            
  • doHighBackgroundJob : Call the job without waitting for result on High Preference.
    • It recieves a job handle for submitted job
    • 
                            
  • doLowBackgroundJob : Call the job without waitting for result on Low Preference.
    • It recieves a job handle for submitted job
    • 
                            
  • callJob : Call the job with default method
    • Defined in settings, work annotations or job annotations
    •

By reading Gearman documentation you can check the method returning data
